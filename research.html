Research

I am currently an undergraduate researcher and have worked on broad range of topics such as - Marginalized and Under-resourced Communities, ICT4D, Child-Robot Interaction (CRI), Ethics. Below is the list of some of my current and past projects.

  • Ethics of CRI: Oct 2020 - Present

    The aim of this project is to explore a what kind of ethical issues arises if we deploy a social robot in an under-resourced community in India. We are analyzing the pre-recorded videos of one of our previous studies done at a community school in New Delhi, India (read here). More details to be added soon.

    Team: Deepa Singh (PhD Scholar, Delhi University)
    Advisors: Dr. Manohar Kumar, Dr. Jainendra Shukla

  • Design for the Well-Being of LGBTQIA+ University Students: Aug 2020 - Present

    Designing for the LGBTQIA+ community in India with a focus on health very much understudied. In India, the LGBTQ+ community is very diverse, and prior research in Sociology has suggested that the community has faced a lot of oppression, discrimination, and violence in society. In this work, I am trying to understand how do LGBTQIA+ university students socialize and seek social support. We aim to answer two broad research questions:
    a) Can technology really offer a solution to address the emotional needs of the community?
    b) If yes, what is the right approach to design, i.e., do we design ’for’ or do we design by involving the community and by leveraging existing human and material assets from ’within’ the community?
    (Read more)

    Advisor(s): Dr. Venkata Ratnadeep Suri, Dr. Jainendra Shukla
    (The work is being done as my Bachelor's Thesis)

  • Predicting Anxiety in Adults using EDA and HRV Signals: Feb 2020 - Present

    The aim of this project is to develop a MachineLearning(ML) model using both EDA & HRV signals, to predict patterns of anxiety in adults. Novelty of this works lies in thefact that there are no pre-existing model in ML which uses these two modalities to deduce anxiety.
    Team : Pramil Pranjawani (Ph.D Scholar, IIIT Delhi), Shubhangi Butta (Undergrad IIIT Delhi)
    Advisor(s) : Dr. Jainendra Shukla, Dr. Mrinmoy Chakrabarty .
